'Siyam World' Indroduces adorable
pony residents..

2025-09-18
share
Save
Rushdi Abualouf

Siyam World Maldives has been pushing boundaries and creating new benchmarks for
the industry ever since it first opened.

The island resort, which is at best a game-changer,
is home to the first and largest horse ranch in the Maldives, which just welcomed three
new members to its local equine family. Three mixed-breed ponies named Cherry, Rose,
and Honey will be joining the lovely mares and strong stallions to provide the young
visitors with a special horse journey.

Pony rides at sunrise and dusk against the stunning
backdrop of the Indian Ocean are now available to young tourists between the ages of 4
and 11 at Siyam World. The three has now been recorded in history as the first ponies
ever to be accepted to a Maldivian resort after the Ministry of Fisheries, Marine
Resources & Agriculture of the Maldives undertook a thorough approval process prior
to their entrance into the flock.

In order to make sure that these gorgeous creatures are
properly acclimated to island life and are able to accept the fully furnished ranch as
their new home, a committed team of skilled hostlers and veterinarians have gone above
and beyond. The entire crew will be under the supervision of a professionally trained
equestrian veterinarian who has moved into the resort permanently, ensuring that no
precaution has been made to ensure the well-being, comfort, and safety of this wonderful
family of Indian horses in the archipelago.
